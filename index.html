Point of Contact

We are contactable via e-mail at noc@jkdev.io. It is monitored at least 18 hours a day, 7 days a week.

If we have messed something up tremendously, please contact us directly there.

If you receive malicious requests from our network or our clients, please send an e-mail directly to abuse@jkdev.io.

Peering

We have an open peering policy and peer at physical IXPs. Check out our PeeringDB entry to see up-to-date information.

Our peering requirements are:

  • Public ASN and a PeeringDB entry for it
  • BGP-capable router connected to a physical IXP's peering VLAN
  • RPKI-filtering preferred, at least IRR filtering
  • Have a reasonably reachable POC, at least a NOC e-mail address
  • No Tunnels, without exceptions

If you wish to proceed, send us an e-mail at noc@jkdev.io.

If we are already peers over IXP route-servers, please also include why you want to establish a direct BGP session.
